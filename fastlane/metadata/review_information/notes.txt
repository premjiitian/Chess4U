Chess4U is a chess training app. No account or login is required — all data is stored locally on device.

To review the app:
1. Complete the onboarding assessment (select any skill level / tap "Skip" for guest mode)
2. From the Train tab, check the Coach Alert banner at the top (appears when a weakness is detected)
3. Explore any of the 8 training modes — tap a card in the Training grid
4. Play a game on the Board tab
5. On the Dashboard, scroll down to see the Focus Areas card (shows weakest puzzle themes)
6. On the Profile tab, scroll to see Puzzle Theme Accuracy and Opening Mastery sections
7. View achievements on the Profile tab or via the Achievements link
