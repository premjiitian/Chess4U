Welcome to Chess4U 1.0!

Chess4U is your personal AI chess trainer — adaptive, data-driven, and built to target the exact weaknesses holding your game back.

What's included in this launch:

• 8 specialized training modes: Tactics, Openings, Middlegame, Endgame, Calculation, Variations, Game Analysis, and Blunder Reduction
• Adaptive AI coach with real-time guidance tuned to your Elo band
• Coach Alert — detects your worst puzzle theme and prompts a targeted fix session instantly from the Training hub
• Focus Areas card on your dashboard — surfaces your two weakest puzzle themes with accuracy data and direct practice links
• Opening Mastery tracker — records W/D/L for every opening you practice, with a segmented score bar and FIDE score%
• Puzzle Theme Accuracy — ranked breakdown of all themes you've attempted, worst first
• Skill rings and radar chart for at-a-glance progress
• Daily puzzle with its own streak counter
• Weekly training plan generated by the Tree-of-Thought engine
• Achievements and training streak to keep you motivated
• WidgetKit home-screen widget showing your current streak
• Full VoiceOver accessibility support
• All data stored on-device — no account required
